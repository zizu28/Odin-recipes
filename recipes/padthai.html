PADTHAI

This is a common delicacy in Thailand usually supplemented with ground chilli and sugar (optional). This food is known worldwide and popular among tourists in Thailand.

Ingredients

  • Thai noodles
  • Peanuts
  • Eggs
  • Bean shoots
  • Ground Chilli
  • Seafood variety

Steps

  1. Boil noodles for 15 minutes.
  2. Fry eggs and scramble them when done.
  3. Add boiled noodles to scrambled eggs.
  4. Stir fry mixtures and add vegetables
  5. Add spices.
  6. Serve with bean shoots, ground chilli and sugar.
  7. Enjoy your meal.
